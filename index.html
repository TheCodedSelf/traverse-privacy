Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted by the Traverse app for iOS and any other devices or platforms.

Information we collect

All Traverse users operate anonymously, without us tracking email addresses or passwords. GPS data of your activities is stored in the app on your device only.

Third party integrations

If you choose to connect Traverse to Strava, we store some of your Strava data locally on your device. We also store tokens to keep you logged in to Strava. All of your Strava data is deleted the moment you disconnect Traverse from Strava.
